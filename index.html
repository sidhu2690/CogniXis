Welcome to CogniXis

CogniXis is a dedicated blog that embarks on a deep journey into the world of machine learning, with a special focus on deep learning. Here, we delve into the mathematical foundations and advanced concepts that power modern AI—from linear algebra to state-of-the-art neural network architectures.

Explore topics ranging from the basics of regression and optimization to sophisticated models like Graph Neural Networks, Recurrent Neural Networks, Transformers, and more. Each post is designed to take you step-by-step through the theory, math, and practical implementations behind these cutting-edge techniques.
